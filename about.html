About Danielle

Hi there! I am a long time sewist living in the National Capitol Region of Canada. I am a self taught sewist who eventually started working at a local fabric store in Michigan (Seams Fabric) where I really honed in my sewing skills! Eventually I also started teaching group classes and private classes. When I moved to the Ottawa area I worked at Fabrications Ottawa and taught a couple classes with them as well. Eventually I decided I would like to do some more schooling though, so now I am teaching private lessons while also attending college.
