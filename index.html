Welcome

I'm so excited you're here!

For me sewing means creativity, excitement, and feeling good about me and I am passionate about helping others find their joy in sewing too. The best way for me to achieve that is with personalized, private lessons designed just for you! Whether you're a beginner eager to learn the basics or an experienced sewist looking to refine your skills, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

In my lessons, I'm going to help you build the skills and confidence to tackle a wide variety of sewing projects, from beginner-friendly designs to more advanced creations, so you can sew with ease at any level. No matter your skill level, I will tailor each session to meet your needs and help you achieve your sewing goals.

Sewing Can Be For Everyone!

Alright, maybe not for pets. However I believe that sewing is empowering for all ages and genders. Being able to feel good in what you wear (because you made it just for you) or because crafting is good for the mind! I'm here to help people get their sewing journey started.

Send me a message to start your sewing adventure!
